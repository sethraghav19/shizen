Shizen Journey

Our Engineering Timeline

Drag or scroll horizontally to follow our evolution from a Tokyo tech incubator to a global heavy-lift green pioneer.

2021

The Foundation

Shizen is established in Tokyo, Japan. Pioneering clean hybrid hydrogen power systems specifically engineered for high-torque heavy-duty transports.

2022

Active Adapter Lift

Developed the revolutionary Shizen BladeLifter-S85. Enabling wind turbines to navigate deep mountain corridors by rotating rotor blades dynamically.

2023

Antwerp Hub Expansion

Established our major European Operations terminal in Antwerp, Belgium. Commenced high-precision roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) offshore load-outs.

2024

100% Zero-Emissions PPU

Engineered and deployed a fully autonomous hydrogen-fuel-cell Power Pack Unit (PPU), removing all diesel-hydraulic generators from our SPMT fleet.

2025

Global 3D Corridor Twin

Completed scanning and digitization of over 10,000 kilometers of heavy-haul transport routes, building a comprehensive 3D physics digital twin network.
